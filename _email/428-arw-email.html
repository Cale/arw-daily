Editor's Corner

Hi, my name is Cale (K4HCK) and I'm the editor of Amateur Radio Weekly.

The most-clicked link from last week's newsletter was VE9KK's adventure locating and removing RFI in the shack.

The moment I saw video of the Empire State Building climbers, I was concerned they were going to become victims of serious RF burns from the extremely powerful antennas on the spire. It turns out there are protocols in place to halt transmission when people are present. It's a fascinating safeguard. I just wish the RadioWorld article went into more detail.
