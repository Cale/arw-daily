[QRZ.com](https://www.qrz.com/) has announced the [USA 250 Award](https://forums.qrz.com/index.php?threads/announcing-the-usa250-award.976740/) to celebrate the 250th anniversary of the founding of the United States of America. Participants are encouraged to make 250 confirmed QSOs with US based amateurs throughout 2026. The award is open to all ham radio operators across the globe.

Additional details are available on the [QRZ forums](https://forums.qrz.com/index.php?threads/announcing-the-usa250-award.976740/).
