[AMSAT Switzerland](https://www.amsat-hb.org/) has announced a special event and [contest](https://www.amsat-hb.org/hb9rg_trophy/hb9rg_trophy_2026) that encourages hams to make extreme long-distance contacts via amateur satellite. The event recognizes Dr. Hans Rudolf Lauber (HB9RG) and his contribution to amateur radio.
