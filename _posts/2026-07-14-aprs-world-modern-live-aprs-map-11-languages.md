*The following is a message from TA4OHN:*

[aprs.world](https://aprs.world/) is a free, modern web map of the worldwide APRS network — a fresh alternative to the classic trackers. Live APRS-IS firehose, station pages for every callsign, weather and telemetry graphs, and two-way APRS messaging straight from the browser. The interface is available in 11 languages, no login required for the map.

There's also an [Android companion app](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=world.aprs.app) that goes beyond viewing: paired with a Bluetooth KISS TNC, it transmits real APRS packets over RF from your phone.

hamradio.my [covered the project](https://hamradio.my/aprs-world-the-browser-based-aprs-map-thats-changing-how-hams-see-the-world/) in May.
