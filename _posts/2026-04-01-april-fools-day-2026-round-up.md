April Fools' Day wouldn't be complete without several attempts at mischief among the amateur radio community and 2026 has been no exception! Here are links to several April Fools' Day events that made their way across the *Amateur Radio Daily* news desk.

[DX Engineering Unveils a Ham Radio Game-Changer—the All-New Antenna Encabulator](https://www.onallbands.com/dx-engineering-unveils-a-ham-radio-game-changer-the-all-new-antenna-encabulator/)   
After decades of research, DX Engineering has perfected the balance of inverse reactive current and the automatic syncrization of ham radio grammeters thanks to the all-new high-performance Antenna Encabulator. See the [related video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8b1JOgI_p3o).

[The RetroRig by N6ARA Electronics](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=urp9Td6i8Q0)   
RetroRig offers the least-modern discomforts optimized for the traveling ham. [View the video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=urp9Td6i8Q0) or [Place your order](https://n6ara.com/product/retrorig/).

[U.S. Administration Supports Ham Radio Homebrew](https://soldersmoke.blogspot.com/2026/04/us-administration-supports-ham-radio.html)   
In a decision that puzzled many analysts, the Federal Communications Commission has come out with a new policy that seems to support the at home construction ("homebrew") of amateur radio equipment.

[POTA Platinum — new paid tier for POTA!](https://potaplatinum.com/)   
To maximize profits, we're introducing POTA Platinum. New rules, new benefits — for a price. [See this video explanation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5ul9xHvtVoQ).
