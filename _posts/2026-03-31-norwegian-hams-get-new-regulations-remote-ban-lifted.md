Amateur radio operators in Norway have a number of [new regulations](https://www.ssa.se/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Radioamatorforskrifter-2026.pdf) including a new entry-level license, "begrenset lisens." Remote control limitations have been lifted, specifically the limitation over remote operation in Norwegian overseas territories. The 23cm band now more closely adheres to [CEPT](https://www.cept.org/ecc/topics/radio-amateurs) recommendations. 

More details are available from [Föreningen Sveriges Sändareamatörer](https://www.ssa.se/norge-far-nya-amatorradiobestammelser/).
