[RARSfest](https://www.rarsfest.org/), the Raleigh, North Carolina area hamfest is seeking speakers for their April 2026 event.

All ham radio related topics are encouraged with the opportunity to speak in-person during 50 or 80 minute time slots. Proposals should be emailed to [rarsfest@rars.org](mailto:rarsfest@rars.org) by 5:00 PM EST on Monday, February 16th, 2026.

The hamfest takes place on April 4th, 2026. RARSfest is sponsored by the [Raleigh Amateur Radio Society](https://www.rars.org/).
