The [League of Brazilian Amateur Radio Transmitters](https://www.labre.org.br/) (LABRE) will [celebrate](https://www.labre.org.br/labre-92-anos-transmissoes-em-sstv-valem-certificado-especial/) its 92nd anniversary through a series of SSTV images transmitted via the [UmKA-1](https://en.spacepi.space/satellites/um-ka-1/) (RS40S) satellite. SSTV transmissions will take place between February 6 and February 9.

- **Frequency:** 437.625 MHz
- **Mode:** Robot36
- **Transmission interval:** 3 minutes

A special certificate will be available for operators that receive the SSTV image. Image files may be [submitted here](https://hampass.com/labre-sstv).
