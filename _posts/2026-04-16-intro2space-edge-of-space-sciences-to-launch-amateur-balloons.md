Edge of Space Sciences ([EOSS](https://www.eoss.org/)) and Intro2Space will [launch a balloon](https://www.eoss.org/node/5885) with an APRS payload on April 18th. The balloon will launch from [Metro State University of Denver](https://www.msudenver.edu/) in Colorado and carry an APRS beacon utilizing callsign AEØSS-4. In addition to the APRS playload, the balloon will carry an ADS-B beacon and sonar experiment.

The predicted flight path can be [viewed here](https://eoss.org/predict/).

A preflight net will take place on the [Fun Machine linked repeater system](https://www.we0fun.com/), TGIF talkgroup 815, and Echolink node CANONCTY.

> Edge of Space Sciences (EOSS) formed in 1990 and has been launching high altitude balloon experiments on behalf of universities, colleges, high schools and middle schools for over 26 years.
