Join [Radio Club de la Sarthe](https://aras72.r-e-f.org/) in celebrating the greatest race in motorsport, the [24 Hours of Le Mans](https://www.24h-lemans.com/en). Special event station [TM24H](https://www.qrz.com/db/TM24H) will be on the air through June 14th to highlight the oldest active endurance race in which teams compete to span the greatest distance over 24 hours.

Look for TM24H on the following modes:

- SSB
- CW
- RTTY
- PSK
- FT8
- VHF & QO-100
