The [Polish Amateur Radio Union](https://pzk.org.pl/) (PZK) is [celebrating](https://ot09.pzk.org.pl/akcje/100gdynia/100gdy.php) the 100th anniversary of the city of [Gdynia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gdynia) in Poland. Between February 7th and 22nd, six unique call signs will be on the air:

- SP100G
- SQ100D
- SO100Y
- SN100N
- HF100I
- 3Z100A

eAwards are available in PDF format as well as a limited number of printed QSL cards.
