*The following is a message from David (VE3KGK):*

From May 10 to May 16 a small group in SW Ontario will be putting on the special call sign [VB3COPS](https://www.qrz.com/db/VB3COPS) during Police Services Week to honor and thank all Police Personnel for what they do for us daily, and from May 17 to May 23 we will be putting on special call sign [VB3EMS]() during Emergency Medical Services Week to honor and thank all our First Responders for the job they do daily for which we seldom get to thank them.

Information will be available on [QRZ.com](https://www.qrz.com/) under [VB3COPS](https://www.qrz.com/db/VB3COPS) and [VB3EMS](https://www.qrz.com/db/VB3EMS).

Logs will be uploaded to [eQSL](https://www.eqsl.cc/) after the end of the events. Paper QSLs will also be available.
