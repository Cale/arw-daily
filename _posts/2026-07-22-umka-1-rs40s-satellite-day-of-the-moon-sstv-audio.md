The UmKA-1 (RS40S) satellite is scheduled to [conduct special transmissions](https://r4uab.ru/2026/07/20/20-26-iyulya-2026-goda-zaplanirovana-speczialnaya-translyacziya-s-mka-umka-1-rs40s/) for [International Moon Day](https://www.un.org/en/observances/moon-day) between July 20th and July 26th. Those listening can expect a mix of special audio clips as well as SSTV images which will alternate day-to-day. The schedule is expected to follow:

- 7/20 SSTV and Audio
- 7/21 Text
- 7/22 Text
- 7/23 SSTV and Audio
- 7/24 Text
- 7/25 SSTV and Audio
- 7/26 Short-term technical broadcast

SSTV will transmit in Robot 36 mode.
