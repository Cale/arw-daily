Colorado Summits On the Air will be active August 1st and 2nd for the [Colorado 14er](https://ham14er.groups.io/g/ham14er/wiki/) Event. Two bonus days will take place July 30 and 31.

> During the Colorado 14er & Mountaintop Event, amateur radio operators from around Colorado will be activating Summits On The Air (SOTA) peaks and communicating with other radio amateurs across the state and around the world.

A [14er Groups.io list](https://ham14er.groups.io/g/ham14er/) is also available.
