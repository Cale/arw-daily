[EvoHam.com](https://evoham.com/) recently [launched](https://evoham.com/evoham-is-live/) as a resource for amateur radio operators interested in digital voice modes. The new site focuses on DMR, D-STAR, Yaesu Fusion, P25, NXDN, M17, and FreeDV with how-to guides, reviews, and tutorials.

> Whether you’re a beginner exploring your first DMR handheld or an experienced operator experimenting with M17 or FreeDV, EvoHam brings everything together in one place – guides, reviews, tutorials, tools, resources, and news.

Example articles include [M17 for Beginners: A Step-by-Step Guide for New Hams](https://evoham.com/m17-for-beginners/) and [FreeDV for Beginners: Get Started with Digital Voice on HF](https://evoham.com/freedv-for-beginners/).
