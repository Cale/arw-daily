The initial [spectrum24 conference](https://spectrum-conference.org/) took place in Rambouillet, France on the 14th and 15th of September 2024. [Hacker Public Radio](https://hackerpublicradio.org/) was there and recently published several interviews from presenters and sponsors.

[Audio of interviews from day 1 is available here](https://hackerpublicradio.org/eps/hpr4213/index.html) and includes:

- Sylvain Azarian (F4GKR) president of the International [Amateur Radio Union (IARU) Region 1](https://www.iaru-r1.org/)

- Anthony Le Cren (F4GOH) and his effort to set up an amateur radio station at a technical school
