[Amateur Radio on the International Space Station](https://www.ariss.org/) (ARISS) was the first educational payload on the International Space Station (ISS). As a result, ARISS is celebrating 25 years of the ISS with an [SSTV event](https://mastodon.hams.social/@ARISS_Intl/115486884397386619) to take place November 12th through the 20th. The series will also celebrate Scouting.

Series 30 will consist of 12 different images transmitted every 2 minutes on 145.800 MHz. Follow [ARISS on social media](https://mastodon.hams.social/@ARISS_Intl) for additional details and updates.
