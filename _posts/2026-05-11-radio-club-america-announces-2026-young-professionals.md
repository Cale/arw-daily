[Radio Club of America](https://radioclubofamerica.org) (RCA) has Announced their 2026 Young Professionals, honoring 5 industry innovators under the age of 40. The 5 recipients are Precious Fodor, Andy Huyhn (KA6NDY), Max Johnson, Sean Martin, and Chris Vargas. Their experiences range from Land Mobile Radio to broadcast engineering.

Learn more about the 2026 Young Professionals on the [RCA's website](https://radioclubofamerica.org/content.aspx?page_id=22&club_id=500767&module_id=512350).
