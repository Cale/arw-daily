Registration is now open for the 2026 [RaDAR Rally](https://www.radarrally.info/) event. RaDAR is Rapid Deployment Amateur Radio, an event in which ham radio operators deploy into the field, make five contacts, then redeploy again all within a 4 hour window. 

See the RaDAR Rally info page for [registration details](https://www.radarrally.info/2026/02/registration-is-open-for-april-4th-2026.html). The event takes place April 4th with registrations accepted through April 3rd. An [intro video](https://youtu.be/moHlgtAkkJ0?si=oqpudWG-o7VwDg2O) and [roster](https://www.radarrally.info/p/radar-rally-roster.html) are also available.
