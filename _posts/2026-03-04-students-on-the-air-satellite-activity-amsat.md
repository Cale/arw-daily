AMSAT recently [announced](https://amsat-uk.org/2026/03/01/students-on-the-air-satellite-activity-starts-march-3/) Students On The Air (StOTA) Days in which students are invited to get on the air and work satellites the first and third Tuesday of each month.

> The activity was inspired by AMSAT President Drew Glasbrenner, KO4MA, and his son Carsten, KQ4SJM, and is designed to promote regular on-air activity among licensed student operators.

All operators are encouraged to participate. More information is available on the [AMSAT Discord](https://discord.gg/xbTXcPJHyt) server.
