*The following is a message from the [International Amateur Radio Association](https://www.aira.pt/) (AIRA):*

On July 18, 2026, from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, [AIRA](https://www.aira.pt/) will be [activating the Barra Lighthouse in Ílhavo, Aveiro- Portugal](https://www.aira.pt/pt/events/ativacao-farol-barra-2026). We warmly invite radio amateurs to join us in this special activation. The designated callsign will be CS5AIRA.

The activity will operate across the 20 and 40-meter bands, as well as VHF and UHF frequencies. Communications will include links via Echolink-LUSOFONA, LART Network, the QO-100 satellite, and DMR via Talkgroup 268 (10:00 - 12:00) and Talkgroup 91 (14:00 - 18:00) on the BrandMeister platform.

See the [event website](https://www.aira.pt/pt/events/ativacao-farol-barra-2026) for more information.
